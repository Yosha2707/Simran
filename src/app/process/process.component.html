Customer Process

SIMRAN FRESH

Ordering:

We accept Orders via our Website, Mobile Applications, Whatsapp and On Call Customer Care

Procurement

Our Expert and Experienced Sourcing team procures best quality products from Farmers and Markets.

Pre Cleaning

Almost all the products are pre cleaned and pre washed to remove any physically placed harmful foreign particles from the surface of Fruits and Vegetables.

Ozonization

The cleaned Products are again washed using pressured Ozonized water. Usually the fruits and vegetables we purchase may look neat and safe, but many times they contain residues of toxic pesticides, or get contaminated with bacteria and other harmful microbes due to unhygienic storage or transportation. Ozone being a powerful food grade disinfectant is capable of removing almost all bacteria and neutralizing harmful pesticides from the outer surface.

Sorting

Under the experienced eyes of our team, all the products are sorted for only the Best Quality produce and then are further sorted into different sizes and weights.

Packing

In a hygienic environment, the Products are scaled on high grade electronic weighing scale and then packed & sealed in high quality bags, which are purely decomposable to ensure germ-free packing

Quality Control

We carefully evaluate each and every fruit and vegetable we sell at every stage from Procurement to Packaging.

Delivery

The Products are properly arranged in cloth-bags as per Orders and arranged in temperature controlled and germ-free Delivery Boxes. A well-trained Delivery Agent then takes the Orders to the Customers

Payment

The Payment is either made online in advance or collected at the door-steps. The Delivery Agent collects Payment details from the Customer
